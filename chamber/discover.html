Plaza de La Merced

First called Plaza Mayor, it currently occupies the same land as in its beginnings (1750). The importance of this square is that it was the component by which the formation of the town was guided. Around the square the Fort, the Chapel, the Parish Cemetery, the Posta were built. Around it, the first streets were opened and the first adobe and straw houses were built. Over the years, this has fulfilled the real function of a plaza, functioning as a center for official ceremonies and a place for community meetings.
