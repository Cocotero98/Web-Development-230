Site Purpose

The objective of the Pergamino Chamber of Commerce is to promote and publicize local businesses and our city's main attractions. We want to help all of our affiliates grow bigger so, we provide several benefits for them. For example, we include them in our directory and show their information to our visitors. In this directory, we also have the main attractions of the city. Besides, we count with a section showing all the local culture and history. We provide a link and contact information for those who want to become affiliates and join our organization.
