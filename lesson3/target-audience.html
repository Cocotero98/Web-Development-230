Target Audience

The website is mainly for those looking for information about the chamber. Some will want to know what a chamber of commerce is. Others will be looking for information about the benefits of joining or how they can do it. Besides, there will be people looking for information about the city. Most of them are adults, between 20 and 50.

Persona 1

Ismael Acosta

  • Occupation: Owner at Acosta's chickens
  • information: 48 years old. Married. Father of two children. He has many chicken stores across the city. Has a Bachelor's Degree in agribusiness.
  • Goals and motivation for using the site: He wants to know how being a member of the chamber of commerce can benefit him.
  • Devices: Most of the time uses his phone. At times uses the computer for specific tasks.
