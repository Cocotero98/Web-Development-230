Repetition

Family Search

familysearch.org

FamilySearch is a good example of the repetition design principle. It uses the same header and footer on every subpage. It also repeats the same colors, fonts, and style. Because of this, we can see a relationship between its elements.

White space

Mercado Libre

mercadolibre.com

Mercado Libre is a good example of using white space between the elements. Because of the white space, it looks more organized and is more understandable. It's easy and comfortable to navigate through the website.

Contrast

Renault

renault.com.ar

In renault.com.ar we can see a defined contrast between its elements. This contrast occurs because of the different colors and font sizes this website uses. It uses mainly black and white to create this contrast.
